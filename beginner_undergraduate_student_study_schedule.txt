    *   **Wednesday (July 15, 2026):**
        *   6:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Introduction to Arrays.
        *   7:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Practice: Array initialization, traversal, finding min/max in array.
    *   **Thursday (July 16, 2026):**

    *   **Wednesday (July 22, 2026):**

        *   7:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Practice: Demonstrate polymorphism with an `Animal` class and subclasses making different sounds.
    *   **Thursday (July 23, 2026):**

    *   **Friday (July 24, 2026):**

    *   **Friday (July 31, 2026):**
